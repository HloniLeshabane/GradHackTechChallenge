Definition of Exposed:

*Exposed in family or community setting is spending >15 minutes in infected
person's company AND being < 1.5m apart AND not wearing a face mask

*Exposed in clinical setting is not wearing proper PPE,or PPE failure AND
spending > 1.5 minutes in infected person's company AND being < 1.5 .
